Michigan, USA

Detroit

It was founded in 1701 by a French trader who built a fort on the river and named it Fort Pontchartrain du Détroit.
Later the British just called it Detroit. In the 20th century the city’s name became synonymous with the American
automotive industry, as the home of the Big Three.

About

Weather

In Detroit, summers are hot and winters are cold. Some Michiganders like to experience all four seasons, while others
could go for one or the other.

Population

Detroit is the largest and most populous city in Michigan. The metropolitan area is home to 4.3 million people.
Regarded as a major cultural center, Detroit is known for its contributions to music, art, architecture and design.

Sports

Football is said to be the most popular sport in Detroit but it is also home to the Detroit Pistons, Tigers and Red Wings.
